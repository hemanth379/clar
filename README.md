# Clar — Clarity for your day

An AI-powered task management app that turns plain English into structured, 
actionable tasks. Type how you think — Clar handles the rest.

🔗 **Live:** [clar-six.vercel.app](https://clar-six.vercel.app)

---

## What it does

Instead of filling out forms and dropdowns, you just type:

> *"submit project report next Friday at 9am"*

Clar parses your input, extracts the date, sets the priority, and generates 
a clear action plan — automatically, in under a second.

---

## Features

- **Natural language task creation** — AI parses free-text input into structured tasks with dates, priorities, and action steps
- **Three views** — List, Kanban, and Calendar. Switch anytime, data stays the same
- **Projects** — Group tasks into separate spaces for work, personal, and side projects
- **Smart reminders** — Email and browser notifications when tasks are due
- **Google Calendar sync** — Tasks with due dates automatically appear in your Google Calendar
- **Real-time sync** — Every change syncs instantly across all tabs and devices
